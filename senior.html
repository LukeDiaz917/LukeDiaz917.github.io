Senior Design Project

Problem Statement

Describe the real‑world need, users/stakeholders, constraints (technical, budget, schedule), and success criteria. Summarize prior art and why existing solutions fall short.

  • Users & Context: Who benefits and in what scenarios.
  • Requirements: Must‑have functional and non‑functional goals.
  • Metrics: How success is measured.

Preliminary Design Review (PDR)

Document concept studies, tradeoffs, early analyses/tests, and initial risk assessment. Include preliminary schematics, CAD, and BOM estimates.

  • System concept & block diagram
  • Key trade studies & rationale
  • Prototype/bench tests & results

Critical Design Review (CDR)

Present finalized design, detailed drawings/schematics, interface definitions, manufacturing plan, verification plan, and mitigations for top risks.

  • Detailed architecture & interfaces
  • Manufacturing/assembly plan
  • Verification & validation plan

Final Presentation

Summarize outcomes, performance vs. requirements, lessons learned, and future work. Embed or link your slide deck and demo video.

  • Results & performance metrics
  • Demo highlights
  • Future improvements

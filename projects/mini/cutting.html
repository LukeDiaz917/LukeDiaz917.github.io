Process

  1. After cutting, I peeled away the excess vinyl carefully so only the design remained. Using a pick tool helped lift out the small interior pieces without tearing the design.
  2. I pressed transfer tape over the weeded design to lift it up in one piece. Then I aligned it on the surface, smoothed it from the center outward with a squeegee, and slowly peeled away the tape to leave a clean decal.

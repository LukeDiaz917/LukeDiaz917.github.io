Artificial Reef — Project Hub

Central page for the capstone: Problem Statement, PDR, CDR, and Final Presentation. Add images, videos, and PDFs as you go (drop files into /assets/ and link them here).

Problem Statement

Most existing reef projects lack continuous, site-specific monitoring systems, leaving managers without the data needed to make timely, informed decisions that could keep reefs healthy.

Preliminary Design Review (PDR)

Concept variants (modular blocks, 3D-printed forms, recycled materials), trade studies, risk register, and initial validation plan (tank tests, CFD, materials testing).

Critical Design Review (CDR)

Finalized architecture and drawings (dimensions, assembly, deployment plan), bill of materials, test procedures (flow, scour, durability), and safety/environmental compliance.

Final Presentation

Results, measured outcomes (species counts, colonization rates, flow patterns), lessons learned, and next steps.
